AlphaMedic


The project is a WEB-service, which gives functionality for automatization of hospital work. By using this service, the patients can register to receptions, examination, etc. in comfortable time for them for avoiding queues and choose doctor if they want. Also they can review their medical history, list of drugs for healing etc. The Medical personal can review their organizer, use flexible tool for documentation the medical history of patient and delegate the process of healing to other doctors.
